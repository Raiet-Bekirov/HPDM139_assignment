Agenda – Differential Fairness Toolkit Project

Meeting #5 Date: 11th December 2025 Time: 10:30 AM (Online)

1. Welcome and Purpose of Meeting

  • To compare each member’s prototype pipeline.
  • To agree on which components should be merged into the shared toolkit.

2. Review of Individual Pipelines

Each member presents their minimal example, including:

  • Dataset loading method
  • Preprocessing steps
  • Fairness metric implementation
  • Model training/evaluation
  • Output clarity and usability

Discussion points:

  • What worked well?
  • What was confusing or inefficient?
  • Are there common patterns we should standardise?

3. Agreeing the Core Toolkit Design

Based on the pipeline comparison:

  • Confirm folder/module structure
  • Confirm naming conventions
  • Finalise which functions belong in v0.1
  • Identify anything missing (e.g., documentation, examples, tests)

4. Decisions on What to Merge Into dev

  • Vote or discuss which components each person will contribute
  • Define rules for Pull Requests
  • Assign responsibilities for merging and reviewing

5. Next Steps and Work Allocation

  • Who will implement the dataset loader?
  • Who will implement metric functions?
  • Who will update documentation?
  • Who will build example notebooks?
  • Who will test installation and imports?
